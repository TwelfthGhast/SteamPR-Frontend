What is PageRank?

The PageRank algorithm was initially designed to use with search engines
to determine which websites were the most trustworthy or relevant based
on the hyperlinks between them. SteamPR implements this considering steam
accounts as 'websites' and friend connections as 'hyperlinks'. Currently
the algorithm still needs to be tweaked for Steam Accounts.

Why choose SteamPR?

While similar to other steam ranking websites, SteamPR is different
in that it ranks accounts from a mix of factors including steam level
and friends using a modified PageRank formula. The SteamPR database
is updated on a weekly basis - however, you can still check basic
information even when you aren't in the database; just not rankings!
